## About Giggle

**Giggle** is a flexible work platform that connects people with daily job opportunities — fast, simple, and without the hassle of traditional hiring. Whether you're looking for extra income, a fresh start, or your next big opportunity, Giggle puts work within reach starting from **tomorrow**.

We believe everyone deserves access to work that fits their life — not the other way around. With Giggle, job seekers can browse and accept shifts on their own terms, while employers get reliable workers when they need them most.

## What We Do

- **Match workers with employers** in real time
- **Flexible daily shifts** — you choose when and where you work
- **Get started fast** — from sign-up to first shift in no time
- **Support for workers and businesses** across multiple industries
